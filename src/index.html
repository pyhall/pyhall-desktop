Hall is set up.
Your vault is locked and your session is active. Here's what to do next.
Enroll a worker
Workers are the governed agents Hall routes work to. Drag a registry_record.json into the Enroll screen, or use the CLI to scaffold one:
hall scaffold --id wrk.yourname.worker-name
Connect your tools
Hall can govern any MCP server — your own tools, third-party APIs, anything. Add them in Settings → Connected MCPs. Hall proxies every tool call through the governance gate.
Ready to go live
Click Go Online on the Status screen to start dispatching. Hall checks your account standing with the registry and activates.
